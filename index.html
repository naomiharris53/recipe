Reasons why you should eat a sno cone:

  • There is flavor in every taste bud.
  • They are not always full of sugar.
  • They can be healthy for you if you please.
  • They can be made on a budget.

How to make:

Step 1:

Gather Materials

  • Blender
  • Amount of ice desired
  • Desired Flavor
  • Toppings (if desired)
  • Cup or Cone
  • Scoop or Spoon

Step 2:

Crush ice in blender & Remove ice from blender

Step 3:

Remove ice from blenderScoop ice into cup & and Add syrup to ice

Step 4

Scoop ice into cup

Step 5:

Add syrup and flavor to ice

Step 6:

Add toppings and ENJOY
